The Designer,

Sher Li Tan

I'm an Industrial Designer who's always thinking beyond the box and mapping the experiences of people and creation processes. As an individual who loves storytelling, I always look beyond products for their story and the inspirations that they originate from. I'm always surprised by the rich patchwork behind a product, such as Marc Newson's bizarre Lockheed Lounge and Jacobsen's simple chairs which have inspired many.

Similarly, I hope to be able to express a unique story for each of my designs. Milim is the vehicle which allows me to reflect on past experiences and translate them into fresh and exciting concepts that the future [users] can experience. I have utilised rapid sketching, solid modelling and hands-on prototyping to realise this aim, but I'm the best at communicating and pitching ideas with visual storytelling and surefire confidence.

Currently, my goals are to gain industry-paced experiences, get acquainted with on-site manufacturing processes and to connect with all who'd love to have a chat about their interests. I hope to learn about you soon!
